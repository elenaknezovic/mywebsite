About me

  • My beginnings were modest. I remember finding about UI/UX design watching videos on the subject. The video was explaining analogy between the two terms. They explained process from everyday life such as eating cereals as UI/UX, which would mean that cereals are the product, a spoon user interface and eating user experience. Watching this video my joy increased and I continued to explore, which led me to discover Udemy site which provided a wide range of courses. On the Udemy site, I began my first courses, XD Beginner Course, Beginner Illustrator, Beginner Photoshop by creator Daniel Walter Scott. Watching them, I decided, to try the tools myself. I soon realized it was a good way to express my creativity, so I continued my education by watching a course on logo design. Realizing that the concept of UX was not clear to me, I visited the Pluralsight page which filled my knowledge gaps. After a while I came to find out that my hobby had turned into a passion and that I would like to pursue it.
